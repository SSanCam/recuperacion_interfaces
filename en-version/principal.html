Adopt a Friend

Among the many tasks carried out in an animal shelter is finding responsible families for each and every animal that arrives at our facilities. This requires a thorough dedication from all the people who live with them daily, so that potential adopters can be provided with detailed information about their temperament and everything that might be decisive when considering whether we want or can take care of that life. Considering that a dog or cat can live an average of fifteen years, anything that leads us to make the right decision will always be important. You must be absolutely sure that you can take care of them for their entire life.
